How Does This Process Work?

Let us walk you through it.

  • Getting Started

    Pop In Or Send Us An Email

    To get your project started, we need to know what you need done, and what kind of car that you have!
    It's always better to see your project in person, but you can also send us a descriptive email with your VIN number, pictures and what you are hoping to have done.

  • Estimating

    We'll Get You An Estimate

    After seeing your project, we'll be able to provide you with an estimated price of how much your project will cost, as well as a rough timeline.
    If we can see your project in person rather than over email, we will be able to provide a more accurate estimation of the project's price.
    If you decide to go with us, we can now get your project on our schedule.

  • Working

    Let The Project Begin

    Once your project has been dropped off or delivered, we will start working on it. We will keep you in the loop every step of the way so that you know exactly what's happening and what to expect.

  • Pick up

    Delivery Day

    When your project is nearing completion, we will be in contact to arrange a day for pickup. We pride ourselves on a high standard of work, so if there is anything that you are unhappy with, please let us know so that we can fix any issues. We want your project to look better than when it rolled off of the showroom floor!

1972 Chevy Blazer

Custom Green

This awesome truck came to us in really rough condition after having been 'repaired' by another shop. The frame and body needed to be straightened, rusted panels needed replacing and repair and the truck needed a new coat of paint. The owner decided on a custom forest green paint with copper flake and even had the roll-bar and rims powder-coated a burnt bronze color and had the interior bed-lined.
